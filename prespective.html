Welcome to Prospective Care

We deliver the care and assistance to fulfil your requirements to get what you want. “achieve your goals and meet your needs”

What we offer?

To enhance the unique personality you have, Prospective Care serve the holistic care and support to the participants that are with NDIS. We provide the services that help to ensure all your support needs are met, to enhance your day-to-day life. We deliver the consistent care, skill development strategies and community involvement activities to boost the enjoyment in your life.

What we do?

We help people with different disabilities to have comfortable and supportive living through the services of individualized care and by addressing their unique requirements.

Why we stand out? Tell me more.

We have a 24 -hour service of care and support with highly qualified staff; an accessible location of residence near the city. We organize various daily activities to boost your energy and mental health; including arts, gym sessions and outings. Services include in-home podiatrist, doctors, physiotherapists, psychologists, occupational therapists and registered nurses making sure that your physical as well as psychological needs are met. Social activities include gatherings, cultural integration, movie nights, and church sessions to address the religious beliefs and practices.

Our Services

We have 24/7 care and support services with highly qualified staff; an accessible location of residence near the city. We organize various daily activities to boost your energy and mental health; including arts, gym sessions and outings. Below are some of the services that we provide.

Independent living support

We offer a long term living accommodation and assistance in either client’s choice of living place or in our facilities. We strongly support you living and wellbeing in the place you find most comfortable.

Respite care

We have short term (Respite) accommodation services in our facilities to support the need of you or your family. Short term accommodation assistance is the care just like in your home but away from your home.

Coordinate support service

We as a NDIS support provider can coordinate between the agencies to offer best possible service assistance you can have. We offer and assist you a long term solution to your challenges.

Social participation support

We provide a network of support that assist our participants to get out and engage in the community more. We offer you a confident boost to get more outdoor and live your life in a normal way.

Religious support

We respect people from all religious backgrounds and we aspire to cater for your needs by taking you to the places of worship. we value your religious beliefs and offer you freedom to practice them.

Recreational activities and outings

We involve you in recreational activities. Our clients love Barbeque outing, Hiking trips, Fishing, Swimming, Movies, attending Footie games and site seeing.

Transportation

We offer a transport services to overcome your transportation challenges. We keep up with your entertainment needs by taking your outing and involving in recreational activities.

Training, Education and skill developments

We address your training needs where you think is the gap. we strive to enhance your technological skill to adopt with advanced life style.

MISSION VISSION AND
VALUES

At Prospective Care we possess following mission, vision and values.

Our mission:

As a NDIS service provider, Prospective care’s mission is to enhance participant’s quality of life by supporting their individual needs, to provide unique opportunities by holistic care and to hand the power to shape their lives in the way they want. Our mission is to ensure that all your support needs are met, to enhance your day-to-day life.

Our Vision:

As a NDIS service provider, Prospective care’s vision is to create best possible service environment for the participants and the care staff to generate the outcomes that includes everyone in Prospective care family. We aim to achieve this goal by ongoing training and assessment.

Our Values:

Integration: We possess the quality of being honest with our clients and having a transparency in our processes. Collaboration: We work together as not only as a team in our network but as a family to support each other’s back. Accountability: We are responsible for the care and support needs of our clients. Respect: We respect the diverse community and acknowledge the forms of disabilities you have. Enjoyment: We strive to make our environment filled with fun activities and our culture inclusive of everyone’s happiness. Therefore, Prospective care as an organisation we value and always keep in our mind that iCARE for my client. Come and join our Prospective Care family, where iCARE for your betterment through our support network.
